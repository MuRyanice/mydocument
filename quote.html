Privacy Policy - Motivational Quotes Club Chrome Extension

Last Updated: December 4, 2024

Introduction

Motivational Quotes Club ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension ("Extension", "Service") handles information when you use it.

Information We Collect

We Do NOT Collect Any Personal Information

Important: Our Extension does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data.

Local Storage Only

The Extension uses Chrome's local storage API (chrome.storage.local) to store the following data exclusively on your device:

  1. Favorite Quotes - IDs of quotes you have marked as favorites
  2. Daily Quote Data - The current day's quote and date (to ensure consistency throughout the day)
  3. Theme Preference - Your selected theme filter preference

All of this data remains on your device and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no information to use, share, or sell.

The local storage data mentioned above is used solely to:

  • Remember your favorite quotes
  • Display the same daily quote throughout the day
  • Remember your theme preference

Data Storage and Security

  • Storage Location: All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API
  • Data Transmission: No data is transmitted to any external server
  • Data Security: Data is protected by Chrome's built-in security mechanisms
  • Data Retention: Data persists until you uninstall the Extension or clear your browser data

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

The Extension operates entirely independently and does not connect to any external servers or services.

Permissions Used

storage Permission

Purpose: To save your favorite quotes and daily quote preferences locally on your device.

What it does: Allows the Extension to use Chrome's chrome.storage.local API to store data on your device.

What we store:

  • Favorite quote IDs (strings)
  • Current daily quote and date
  • Selected theme preference

Privacy Impact: None. All data stays on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

chrome_url_overrides Permission

Purpose: To replace your default new tab page with the motivational quotes display.

What it does: Allows the Extension to override the default Chrome new tab page.

Privacy Impact: None. This permission only changes what is displayed in your new tab page. It does not access, collect, or transmit any data.

Children's Privacy

Our Extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any information at all, this is not applicable.

Your Rights and Choices

Access Your Data

You can view the data stored by the Extension by:

  1. Opening Chrome DevTools (F12)
  2. Going to the Application tab
  3. Selecting "Local Storage" under Storage
  4. Finding the Extension's storage entries

Delete Your Data

You can delete all Extension data by:

  1. Uninstalling the Extension (removes all stored data)
  2. Clearing your browser's local storage data
  3. Using Chrome's "Clear browsing data" feature

Disable the Extension

You can disable or remove the Extension at any time:

  1. Go to chrome://extensions/
  2. Find "Motivational Quotes Club"
  3. Click "Remove" or toggle it off

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the Extension's description on Chrome Web Store

Data Breach

Since we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, there is no risk of a data breach involving your personal data.
