Privacy Policy for NewTaber Chrome Extension

Last Updated: March 14, 2024

1. Introduction

NewTaber ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for our Chrome extension.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We want to be absolutely clear about our data practices:

  • We do NOT collect any personal information
  • We do NOT track your browsing history
  • We do NOT store any user data on our servers
  • We do NOT use cookies
  • We do NOT share any information with third parties

3. Local Storage

The only data we store is:

  • User preferences (extension settings)
  • This data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
  • This data never leaves your browser

4. Permissions

Our extension requires certain permissions to function:

  • activeTab: To modify link behavior on current webpage
  • scripting: To implement new tab opening functionality
  • storage: To save your preferences locally
  • contextMenus: To provide right-click menu options
  • host permissions: To function across websites

5. Changes to Privacy Policy

We will update this Privacy Policy as needed. Significant changes will be notified through the extension's update notes.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at: mystartup@126.com
