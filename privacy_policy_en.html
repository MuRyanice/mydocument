HotNews Browser Extension Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 18, 2024

Introduction

HotNews is a simple browser extension designed to provide users with convenient access to trending news. We highly value your privacy, and this privacy policy aims to explain how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

The HotNews extension does not collect or store any personal information. We do not:

  • Collect your browsing history
  • Track your online activities
  • Collect personal identifiable information
  • Use cookies or similar technologies

Third-Party Websites

This extension embeds https://aipedias.com/hotnews/ website in new tabs. Please note that this website may have its own privacy policies and data collection practices, which are beyond our control. We recommend that you read the privacy policy of that website to understand how they handle your data.

Permission Usage

Our extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: This permission is used to interact with the current tab.

Changes

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed about the latest information.
